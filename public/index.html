CS5610 Project - Ads Manager

This is a web application for marketing managers to manage ad campaigns and assign users to specific ad campaigns. Their are two classes of users: managers and general users. Managers can create and manage campaigns, ads and users. They can assign users to specific campaigns and correspond with their users through a messaging system. Users have their accounts created by managers and are granted access to campaigns by their managers. Once they have access to a campaign, they can create and manage ads for that campaign. They also have the ability to message their manager if need be.

To briefly describe the data model of this application, there are 5 objects: managers, users, campaigns, ads and messages. Managers have a one-to-many relationship with users, campaigns and messages. Users have a one-to-many relationship with messages and a many-to-many relationship with campaigns. Campaigns have a one-to-many relationship with ads. CS5610 Web App Maker

This application is designed to make creating websites easy for those with not much experience. Users can create multiple websites. Websites can have multiple pages. Pages can be filled with multiple widgets of different kinds.

Widgets are the heart of the websites. The image to the right is an example of how widgets are use to design pages. Widgets can be images, text, pages, or even formatted html for more experienced users. They can be rearranged, resized or formatted in other ways to the specification of the user.
